## ### Values ~ Who are you? ~ The tactics

* _Statements of ethics, principles, beliefs that are shared among the stakeholders of an organization._

* Empathy: Understanding and addressing the unique challenges faced by elders in adapting to new technologies.
* Accessibility: Ensuring that all technology solutions are user-friendly and inclusive.
* Continuous Learning: Staying updated with the latest technological advancements and caregiving techniques.
* Collaboration: Working together with caregivers, elders, and tech experts to create effective solutions.
* Respect: Honoring the dignity and autonomy of elders in all interactions and solutions provided.

## Goals ~ What are the milestones? ~ The benchmarks, the metrics, KPIs

_Specific, measurable objectives that help track progress toward achieving the mission and vision._

* By Q2 2026, develop and distribute a comprehensive guide for caregivers on assisting elders with technology.
* By Q3 2026, establish a lending library of at least 20 user-friendly tech devices tailored for elders.
* By Q4 2026, conduct monthly workshops and training sessions for elders and caregivers, reaching at least 10 participants.
