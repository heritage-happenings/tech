# Tech Support for the Elderly Organizations

There are a number of organizations dedicated to supporting the elderly with current technology. Here are some of the most prominent ones:

_The interesting question is: Can any of these service actually be useful to our efforts?_

## [Center for Aging Services Technologies (CAST) - LeadingAge](https://leadingage.org/topic/technology-cast/)

* https://leadingage.org/digital-transformation-in-aging-services/
* https://leadingage.org/introducing-tech-to-older-adults/

LeadingAge's Center for Aging Services Technologies (CAST) is leading the charge to expedite the development, evaluation, and adoption of emerging technologies that can improve the aging experience. CAST has become a broad and far-reaching coalition of aging services organizations, technology companies, research universities, and government representatives.

## [OATS from AARP (Older Adults Technology Services)](https://oats.org/)

* One of the largest trailblazers in this space. OATS designs free, highly structured programs to teach older adults how to leverage current digital tools for daily life, finance, and community connection.


## [Senior Planet](https://seniorplanet.org/)

* The flagship national program of OATS. It operates physical and virtual learning hubs featuring hands-on courses covering everything from basic device navigation to advanced creative applications, smart gadgets, and 3D design.


## [Cyber-Seniors](https://cyberseniors.org/)

* An international non-profit that pairs tech-savvy student volunteers with older adults for personalized, one-on-one technology mentorship. They specialize in real-time troubleshooting, smartphone mastery, and web navigation.


## [CTA Foundation (Consumer Technology Association)](https://www.cta.tech/cta-foundation/)

* A public foundation that funds and partners with local programs to deploy modern consumer tech—such as voice-activated smart home hubs, tablet programs, and assistive listening tech—specifically to reduce isolation and enhance independence.


## [GetSetUp](https://www.getsetup.io/)

* A massive virtual peer-to-peer learning platform explicitly designed for older adults. Classes are taught by seniors, for seniors, covering digital wellness, current software tools, and cloud application navigation.


## [USAging](https://www.usaging.org/)

* The national association representing regional Area Agencies on Aging (AAAs). They regularly partner with modern tech providers to distribute smart doorbells, cameras, and voice-assistants directly to seniors to facilitate aging in place safely.
