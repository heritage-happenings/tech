# Tech Support Subcommittee Charter

## Vision — Why We Exist

* Elders well-prepared for cognitive or physical challenges, with technology that enhances quality of life, independence, and social connection
* Caregivers empowered to support elders through technological changes
* A community where technology simplifies rather than complicates daily life

## Mission — What We Do

* Document and share best practices for residents and caregivers supporting elders with technology
* Provide accessible, user-friendly technology solutions tailored to elders' needs
* Establish a lending library of elder-friendly tech devices
* Conduct regular workshops and one-on-one training sessions for elders and caregivers
* Maintain a dedicated email address for support inquiries

## Goals — Milestones & Metrics

* **Q2 2026** — Develop and distribute a comprehensive caregiver guide on assisting elders with technology
* **Q3 2026** — Launch a lending library stocked with elder-friendly tech devices
* **Q4 2026** — Hold monthly workshops reaching at least 10 participants per session
