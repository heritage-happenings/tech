# Catalog with Tech for the Elderly

For devices and specialized technology beyond standard consumer electronics, several companies produce dedicated catalogs that focus on independent living, mobility, and assistive tech.

Here are the most reliable catalogs for 2026:

### **Print & Digital Product Catalogs**

* **[The Wright Stuff - Health Care Products Catalog](https://www.thewrightstuff.com/daily-living-aids-catalog.html)** This is one of the most popular resources for physical and occupational therapy aids. Their 40-page color catalog is updated twice a year (Spring and Fall) and covers everything from high-tech mobility aids to simple kitchen modifications.
* **[Direct Supply - ADL (Activities of Daily Living) Aids](https://store.directsupply.com/catalog/rehabilitation-adl-aids-23300)** While they primarily serve healthcare facilities, their extensive catalog is a gold standard for professional-grade senior equipment. It is particularly useful if you are looking for heavy-duty mobility devices, fall prevention tech, or specialized furniture.
* **[LS&S (Learning, Sight & Sound) Catalog](https://www.lssproducts.com/)** A specialty catalog focused on low-vision and hard-of-hearing technology. They offer a physical catalog featuring talking watches, video magnifiers, amplified telephones, and other sensory-assistive devices.
* **[Silver Star / FirstStreet - Innovative Products for Seniors](https://firststreetonline.com/)** This catalog specializes in "tech that makes life easier." They are well-known for unique devices like the "jitterbug" style simplified electronics, senior-friendly music players, and high-visibility clocks.

---

### **Benefit-Specific Catalogs**

If you have a Medicare Advantage or private health insurance plan, you may have access to **OTC (Over-the-Counter) Catalogs**. These are often mailed annually and allow you to order health-related technology and safety devices for free using plan credits.

* **[CVS OTC Health Solutions Catalog](https://www.cvs.com/benefits)** Many plans use CVS to provide catalogs for blood pressure monitors, pulse oximeters, and bathroom safety equipment.
* **[The Helper Bees - Align Senior Care Catalog](https://alignseniorcare.com/document/2026-otc-catalog/)** A newer provider that focuses on delivering assistive technology and "aging-in-place" kits directly to seniors through insurance benefits.

---

### **Assistive Technology Directories**

* **[ATIA (Assistive Technology Industry Association) Learning Catalog](https://www.atia.org/path-lms/)** Rather than a sales catalog, this is a directory of the latest innovations in the industry. It’s an excellent place to see what "smart home" bundles and AI-driven assistive tools are hitting the market in 2026.
* **[RESNA Product Directory](https://learn.resna.org/catalog)** The Rehabilitation Engineering and Assistive Technology Society of North America maintains a catalog of products that meet specific engineering standards for safety and accessibility.
