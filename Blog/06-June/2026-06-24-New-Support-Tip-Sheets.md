# 2026-06-24 - New Support Tip Sheets

A very major objective with this website is to help team members, friends, and relatives support older adults with technology in a way that is both effective and compassionate.

A good way of doing this is to provide tip sheets or infographics to provide illustrated guidance on how to help older adults with their phones and other technology.

The first set of tip sheets is now available in the `Support-Tips-and-Tricks` folder. The first three sets of tip sheets are:
- [Helping iPhone Users](../Support-Tips-and-Tricks/Helping-iPhone-Users/README.md)
- [Helping Android Phone Users](../Support-Tips-and-Tricks/Helping-Android-Phone-Users/README.md)
- [How to Help](../Support-Tips-and-Tricks/How-to-Help/README.md)
