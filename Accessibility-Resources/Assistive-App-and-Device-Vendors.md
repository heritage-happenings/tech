#### Smart Cane

#### WeAssist

>Professional Visual Assistance Service for the Visually Impaired
>
>WeASSIST is a video call service where you can get visual assistance from our professional assistants at any time.
>
>WeASSIST connects you to a trained professional who interprets what your camera sees and guides you in real time-so you can move, verify, and decide with confidence. Control stays with you.

*https://wewalk.io/en/weassist/
