# Tech Support Subcommittee Charter

## Vision ~ Why do it? ~ The Objective

_The vision is a descriptive picture of a desired future state — Outlining what the organization wants to be_

* Elders well-prepared for cognitive or physical challenges with technology that enhances their quality of life, independence, and social connection.
* Caregivers empowered with the knowledge and resources to effectively support elders in navigating technological changes.
* A supported community environment where technology enhances rather than complicates daily life.

## Mission ~ What do you do? ~ The Strategy

_The mission is the methods and strategies for successfully achieving the vision_

* Document and share best practices for caregivers supporting elders with technology.
* Provide accessible, user-friendly technology solutions tailored to the needs of elders.
* Establish a lending library of user-friendly tech devices tailored for elders.
* Conduct regular workshops and one-on-one training sessions for elders and caregivers.
* Maintain a dedicated email address for support inquiries.

## Values ~ Who are you? ~ The tactics

_The values are statements of ethics, principles, beliefs that are shared among the stakeholders of an organization._

* Empathy: Understanding and addressing the unique challenges faced by elders in adapting to new technologies.
* Accessibility: Ensuring that all technology solutions are user-friendly and inclusive.
* Continuous Learning: Staying updated with the latest technological advancements and caregiving techniques.
* Collaboration: Working together with caregivers, elders, and tech experts to create effective solutions.
* Respect: Honoring the dignity and autonomy of elders in all interactions and solutions provided.

## Goals ~ What are the milestones? ~ The benchmarks, the metrics, KPIs

_The goals are specific, measurable objectives that help track progress toward achieving the mission and vision._

* By Q2 2026, develop and distribute a comprehensive guide for caregivers on assisting elders with technology.
* By Q3 2026, establish a lending library of a variety of user-friendly tech devices tailored for elders.
* By Q4 2026, conduct monthly workshops and training sessions for elders and caregivers, reaching at least 10 participants.
