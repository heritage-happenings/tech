# How to help Read Me

Helping elderly people with their phones is a surprisingly tricky business.

Elderly: many elderly people have a number of cognitive and physical issues that you may not know about.

You are more familiar with the current technology and can navigate it quickly, while they may struggle with basic functions.

You may not be aware of the specific challenges they face, such as vision or hearing impairments, arthritis, or memory issues.

But you can help them by being patient, understanding, and providing clear instructions.

The infographics in this folder are designed to provide you with an overview of how to assist elderly people with their phones more effectively - no matter the manufacturer or model.
