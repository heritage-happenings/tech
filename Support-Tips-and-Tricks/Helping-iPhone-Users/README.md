# Helping iPhone Users

Tips for supporting residents who use an iPhone. Each tip comes as a resident guide to hand out and a staff poster for the helper — see the [main page](../README.md) for how to use them.

## Tips

- **Easier to see** — make text and icons larger and boost contrast across the whole phone. [Resident guide](iphone-easier-to-see-resident-guide.pdf) · [Staff poster](iphone-easier-to-see-staff-poster.pdf)
- **Magnifier** — turn the iPhone into a magnifying glass for the fine print on labels, menus, and mail. [Resident guide](iphone-magnifier-resident-guide.pdf) · [Staff poster](iphone-magnifier-staff-poster.pdf)
- **Full-screen zoom** — magnify everything on the screen at once. [Resident guide](iphone-full-screen-zoom-resident-guide.pdf)
- **Window zoom** — magnify just part of the screen through a movable lens. [Resident guide](iphone-window-zoom-resident-guide.pdf)
- **Steady taps** — settings that forgive shaky hands and accidental repeat taps. [Resident guide](iphone-steady-taps-resident-guide.pdf) · [Staff poster](iphone-steady-taps-staff-poster.pdf)
- **Siri** — place calls, set reminders, send texts, and open apps by voice. [Resident guide](iphone-siri-resident-guide.pdf) · [Staff poster](iphone-siri-staff-poster.pdf)
- **Getting unstuck** — what to do when a screen looks unfamiliar or the phone seems frozen. [Resident guide](iphone-unstuck-resident-guide.pdf) · [Staff poster](iphone-unstuck-staff-poster.pdf)
